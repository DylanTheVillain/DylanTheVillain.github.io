Site Navigation

To navigate the site, simply click on the buttons in the top left corner of the screen. One of the buttons will take you to my FYE blog, so be careful! The button that says content will direct you to the actual meat of the portfolio; this is where I will argue that I have achieved the learning outcomes. The assingments button will bring you to a page that has a series of links that will open a tab with a pdf of the appropriate assingment. Clicking on the portfolio button will return you to this page. Because this page is javascripted, refreshing the page will return you to the base site with only two buttons.
