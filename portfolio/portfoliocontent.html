Assingment 1: Essay on the evolution of an opinion.

The first assingment that we were given was a paper that was intended to document the evolution of my opinion on a particular topic. This assingment helped me achieve the WPA Outcomes Statement because it forced me to introspective that think critically about a specific topic. To write this paper, I had to introduce the reader to a topic and keep their attention as I described what the paper was about and gave them a detailed narrative about my personal views on this topic. This required me to be capable in rhetorical knowledge as well as critical thinking, reading, and writing. Genine Perez also required me to use the MLA format, which required at least some knowledge of writing conventions.

Assingments 3 and 4

Assingments 3 and 4 were important because they taught me more about conventions. The purpose of assingments 3 and 4 was to use the fundamentals of a classical argument to begin to construct an argument. In assingment 3 I had to learn how to create an enthymeme and warrant as well as figure out appropriate grounds, backing, and conditions of rebuttal. This was espeically usefull in the development of my rhetorical knowledge because I had to learn how to construct and argument using a new technique. Assingment 4 required me to expand on assingment 3 by adding an entire thesis to my developing argument. This of course required critical thinking as well as reading and writing skills because the construction of a thesis requires a skillful introduction to a topic ending with a compound-complex sentance that states you position and argument on the subject with no room for interpretation.
