The Presentation

I felt like I had an effective presentation overall. There were some areas where some extra preperation would have helped however. For example, some of the audience may have noticed a slight wavering in my voice at the beginning of my presentation; this is because I did not rehearse for my presentation at all. This is also why I refered back to the computer more that I would have liked. The obvious solution is for me to practice for my presentations more, but there is a deeper problem where if I don't think that the audience and the instructor are going to be very critical of my presentation I don't practice very much. I think that the strengths of my presentation are the quality of my dictation after I got going as well as the asthetics of the power point itself. I also feel that the best aspect of my presentation was that it was essentially a critique on the topic of "The Condor's" presentation; this made it both funny and informative.

Google Power Searching
Image Color filter

Google image search as a color filter functianlity. This allows you to search images and then filter them by color. This allows you to filter images by context as well. For example if you google tesla and apply the red filter, you will get the car but if you apply the black and white filter, you will get pictures of Nikola Tesla.

Limit Search Results by Type

You can filter you search results by content. You can limit your search results to google maps, or videos, or blogs. If you wanted to find the fastest way to get somewhere, it would be beneficial to limit the search results to different types of maps.

Restrict by Filetype

You can limit you search results to just files; you can also refine you searches further by specifying exactly which filetype to use. For example if you want to get just google maps files, you can restrict your search results to the filetype that google maps uses.

Intext operator

You can use the intext operator to require that a specific string be in each search result. You could use this to make finding quotes easier.

Search By Image

If you have an image that you got off the internet, but you don't know where it was taken or who took it. You can use the google search by image functinality to find where a picture was taken and who took it.
